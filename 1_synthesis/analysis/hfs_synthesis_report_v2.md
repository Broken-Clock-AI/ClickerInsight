# HFS Synthesis Report (v2.1)
*Hardened Knowledge Architecture: Final Resolution*

## 1. Executive Summary
This report synthesizes 8 years of incremental game research (2018-2026) into a unified design framework. We have resolved the fundamental tensions between player agency and system automation using modern AI-driven adaptive pacing and Bayesian validation.

## 2. Theoretical Conflict Resolution

### Conflict 1: Interpassivity vs. Intrusive Compulsion
*   **Source A (Fizek/Alharthi):** Idle games should be "Calm Technology," inhabiting the periphery and rewarding absence.
*   **Source B (Larsson/Cutting):** High-value special events and caps create "Demanding" playstyles and "Non-voluntary Pickups," inducing anxiety.
*   **Resolution:** **Adaptive Interpassivity (PDDA-2026).** The system should use Imitation Learning to estimate the player's tolerance for interaction. If "Withdrawal Discomfort" is detected, increase "Worthwhile Interruption" frequency. If "Machine Zone" trance is dominant, increase "Self-Obviating" automation.

### Conflict 2: Greedy Intuition vs. Mathematical Optimality
*   **Source A (Player behavior):** Players naturally choose items with the highest "Rate/Cost" ratio.
*   **Source B (Demaine-2018):** Global optimality requires the "Efficiency Score" ($\frac{y}{x} + \frac{y}{G}$), which accounts for waiting time.
*   **Resolution:** **Guise-System Alignment (Schmalzer-2019).** Designers should provide UI/UX hints that align the player's "Machinic Thinking" with the Efficiency Score (e.g., "Time to Afford" tooltips). This reduces the cognitive planning cost and keeps the player in the optimal growth loop.

### Conflict 3: Bottleneck Frustration vs. Reset Satisfaction
*   **Source A (Tanenbaum):** Bottlenecks lead to "hundreds of dead kittens" and quitting.
*   **Source B (Alharthi):** Resets (NG+) are the primary path to meta-progression and are highly rewarding.
*   **Resolution:** **Bayesian Bottleneck Detection (RuleSmith-2026).** Use simulation-driven fitness to identify "Unresolvable Bottlenecks" (Cost > Capacity) before they occur. Trigger "Reset Preparation" metaphases via narrative prompts (Fiction-as-Retention) to frame the reset as a reward rather than a failure.

## 3. The Hardened Design Principles

### Principle 1: The Machine Zone is the Goal
Design for the "blank hum of computation." Every mechanic must facilitate a trancelike flow where the player becomes the computer's surrogate.

### Principle 2: Self-Obviating Pacing
The game must systematically automate away the need for the player as complexity rises. The reward for effort is the *option* to interact less.

### Principle 3: Telemetry-Driven Pacing
Static $\alpha$ values ($1.15$) are obsolete. Use the Unity Vector-style stack to adjust growth curves in real-time, ensuring the player stays within the "Moderate Balance" of interactivity.
