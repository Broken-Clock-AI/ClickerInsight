# Document Profile: `updated_tome` (Evidence-Backed)

## Key Concepts

*   **Interpassivity:** Deriving pleasure from delegated action (Fizek, 2018).
*   **The Core Seesaw:** Conflict between exponential costs and polynomial production (Pecorella, 2018).
*   **Prestige Loop:** Reset mechanism using fractional exponents (square/cube roots) to manage growth.
*   **Narrative Through Abstraction:** Emergent narrative through mechanics and paradigm shifts (Spiel et al., 2019).
*   **Player-Friendly Design (PFD):** Ethical framework prioritizing player time and well-being.
*   **Derivative-Based Growth:** Tiered generators producing other generators ($x^n/n!$ growth).
*   **Taylor Series ($e^x$):** Mathematical limit of infinite generator tiers.
*   **Interactivity Spectrum:** Fluid movement between active clicking and passive automation (Larsson, 2018).
*   **Intrusive Omnipresence:** The cognitive presence of a game that is always running (Larsson, 2018).
*   **Ambient Gaming:** Games suited for the periphery of attention (Hwang & Melcer, 2025).
*   **The Attention Economy:** Commodification of player time and attention (Pecorella, 2018).
*   **Hybrid Monetization:** Balancing IAPs and Rewarded Ads for sustainable revenue.
*   **Calm Technology:** Designing for disengagement and non-intrusive play.
*   **Data-Driven Development:** Using analytics to tune growth curves and identify churn points.
*   **Progression Wall:** The inevitable point where growth slows to a crawl without prestige.
*   **The Ladder Climbing Effect:** Psychological satisfaction of restarting with a massive power boost.
*   **Bumpy Progression:** Intentional variation in gameplay speed to maintain engagement.
*   **Satire and Origins:** The genre's roots in *Progress Quest* and *Cow Clicker*.
*   **Carpentry (Philosophy):** Doing philosophy through the construction of systemic models.
*   **Ludic Efficiency:** Providing maximum emotional reward with minimum physical input.
