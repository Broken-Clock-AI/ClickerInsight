## 11) Notes on Paywalled Sources

* Some ACM, IEEE, and Springer versions may be paywalled.
* Legal ways to get them:

  * Search title + “pdf”
  * Use Unpaywall
  * Search ResearchGate
  * Check authors’ university homepages
  * Email authors directly (most are happy to provide copies)
