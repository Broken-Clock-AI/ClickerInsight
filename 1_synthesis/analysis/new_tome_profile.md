*   **Idle/Incremental Game:** Core loop of accumulation, growth, and prestige.
*   **Generators:** Basic, derivative-based, and tier boosts.
*   **Multi-Generator Balance:** Trivial dominance vs. shifting priorities.
*   **Advanced Growth Models:** Derivative-based growth and the calculus analogy (`e^x`).

*   **Psychological Hooks:** First five minutes, daily habits, long-term interest, push notifications.
*   **Player Experience (UX):** Simplicity, "live" screen, meaningful choice.
*   **Existential Loops & Critiques:** Capitalist critique, accelerationism, the diminishing player.
*   **Monetization:** Hybrid model (IAPs vs. Ads), rewarded ads.
*   **Player-Friendly Design (PFD):** Respecting player's time and wallet, avoiding pay-to-win.
*   **Data-Driven Development:** Analytics, churn points, KPIs (eCPM, ARPU, LTV).
