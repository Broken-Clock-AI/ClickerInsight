### Part 4: The Business of Waiting: Monetization & Analytics

*   **Chapter 9: Monetization Strategies: IAPs vs. Ads**
    *   Hybrid Monetization: The industry standard.
    *   In-App Purchases (IAPs): Consumables, Non-Consumables, Subscriptions.
    *   In-App Advertising (IAA): Rewarded, Interstitial, Banner, Native Ads.
    *   Pros and Cons of each (e.g., IAP revenue from "whales" vs. ad monetization for all users).
*   **Chapter 10: Ethical Monetization & Player-Friendly Design (PFD)**
    *   Defining PFD: Fairness to consumers and developers.
    *   PFD Criterion 1: Not forcing play (time-locked content, excessive daily loops).
    *   PFD Criterion 2: Not forcing spending (difficulty spikes, unethical matchmaking).
    *   PFD Criterion 3: No unique advantage for spenders (pay-to-win, exclusive cosmetics without free paths).
    *   PFD Criterion 4: Free players can compete in live services (accessible new content, updated tutorials).
    *   Case studies of ethical and unethical monetization (*Path of Exile*, *Warframe*, *Arknights* vs. *World of Tanks*, *Marvel Strike Force*, *Dead by Daylight*).
*   **Chapter 11: Data-Driven Development & Analytics**
    *   Understanding Player Behavior: Usage, duration, churn points.
    *   Identifying Pain Points & Optimizing Monetization (ARPU, LTV, eCPM).
    *   Iterative Design and Development: Agile/Scrum with analytics.
    *   Ad Revenue Benchmarks: Market share by platform/country, eCPM by ad format.
