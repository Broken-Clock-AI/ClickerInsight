# Document Profile: Ad Monetization in Mobile Games - Benchmark Report 2025

*   **Source:** `tenjin_com_blog_ad_mon_gaming_2025_.md`
*   **Original URL:** `https://tenjin.com/blog/ad-mon-gaming-2025/`

---

### **Abstract/Executive Summary**

This report, a collaboration between Tenjin and Clever Ads Solutions (CAS), provides benchmark data for ad monetization in mobile games for 2025, with updates for Q3 2025. It highlights the increasing adoption of "hybrid monetization" (a mix of in-app advertising and in-app purchases) by game publishers, noting that ad revenue continues to be a significant portion of overall revenue despite challenges. The report includes market share data for ad monetization channels and countries from Tenjin's sample, and average eCPM benchmarks broken down by country and format from CAS.AI data. Key data points cover ad revenue share by platform, ad network, and country for iOS and Android across Q1, Q2, and Q3 2025, as well as full-year 2024. It also explains eCPM (effective Cost Per Mille) and details average eCPM for Banner, Interstitial, and Rewarded Ads by country. The report points out a shift in ad revenue share from iOS to Android since Apple's ATT introduction in 2021, and an overall growth in eCPM attributed to hybrid-casual games and increased competition among ad networks.
