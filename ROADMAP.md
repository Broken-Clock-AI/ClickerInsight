# 🗺 Project Roadmap

The development of **The Compounding Illusion** follows the HFS (High-Fidelity Synthesis) lifecycle, bridging the gap between academic research and functional game design.

## 🟢 Phase 1: Knowledge Extraction (Complete)
- **Academic Harvesting:** Systematic collection of 20+ foundational and modern papers.
- **Market Intelligence:** Sentiment analysis of top-performing mobile/Steam titles.
- **Recursive Synthesis:** Creation of the 13-chapter "The Compounding Illusion" tome.
- **Mathematical Ledger:** Extraction of core balancing formulas and scaling models.

## 🟡 Phase 2: Engine Prototyping (In Progress)
- **Vite/TS Scaffolding:** Initial architecture setup with React and Zustand.
- **Large Number Support:** Integration of `break_infinity.js`.
- **Core Loop v1:** Implementation of manual clicks and basic generator tiers.
- **Generator-to-Generator Logic:** Support for higher-tier generators producing lower-tier ones.
- **Recursive Knowledge Integration:** Directly binding GDD variables to research claim IDs.

## 🔵 Phase 3: Formal Hardening & Simulation (Next)
- **Formal YAML Specification:** Moving from JSON to a strict, typed execution registry.
- **Headless Simulation Harness:** A script to simulate 10,000+ hours of gameplay in seconds to verify balance.
- **Asymptotic Stability Pass:** Fine-tuning cost-scaling multipliers (1.12–1.18) based on simulation data.
- **Efficiency Scoring UI:** Surfacing real-time ROI calculations to the player based on the Demaine-2018 greedy metric.

## ⚪ Phase 4: Emergent Systems (Future)
- **Narrative Abstraction Engine:** Procedurally generating lore and flavor text based on emergent game states.
- **Agent-Based Social Simulation:** Simulating player interactions and meta-game market fluctuations.
- **Ethical Monetization Framework:** Implementing "Calm Technology" principles for non-intrusive, player-respecting revenue.
