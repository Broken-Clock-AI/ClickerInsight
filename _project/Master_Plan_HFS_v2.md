# Master Plan of Action: High-Fidelity Synthesis v2.1 (HFS — Hardened)

## **1. Executive Summary**
This plan replaces linear summarization with a **Recursive Knowledge Architecture (RKA)**. It treats the research corpus as a compiled system of claims, ensuring the "Final Tome v4.0" is temporally aware, conflict-resolved, and simulation-verified.

---

## **2. Core Data Model (The Extraction Schema)**

### **2.1 Claim Schema**
Every extracted insight MUST be represented as:
```yaml
Claim:
  id: [Unique ID]
  statement: [Text]
  domain: [economy | pacing | UX | monetization | system | meta]
  level: [micro | system | meta]
  assumptions: [List]
  variables: [List]
  evidence_type: [empirical | theoretical | anecdotal]
  source: [Filename]
  date: [MM/YYYY]
  confidence: [high | medium | low]
```

### **2.2 Ontology Mapping**
Entities extracted must map to:
- **Resource | Generator | Modifier | Time Mechanic | Player Action | Monetization Hook | Feedback Loop | Meta/Aesthetic**

---

## **3. Phase 1: Universal Corpus Re-Atomization**
**Scope:** 20+ Documents (Foundational + 2024-2026 Harvest).

### **Workflow (Triple-Pass Extraction):**
1.  **Pass 1 — Atomic:** Extract definitions/mechanics into the **Claim Schema**.
2.  **Pass 2 — Delta:** Map temporal relationships (Confirms | Extends | Contradicts | Replaces).
3.  **Pass 3 — Precision:** Extract raw math into `math_ledger.md`.
4.  **Pass 4 — Emergent Overflow:** Capture aesthetic/philosophical insights that bypass mechanical ontology.

---

## **4. Phase 2: Multi-Matrix Synthesis**
- **Claim Matrix:** Traceability between claims, sources, and evidence.
- **System Interaction Matrix:** Identifying hidden dependencies and emergent loops.
- **Conflict Matrix:** Resolving Direct Contradictions and Temporal Supersessions.
- **Consensus Scoring:** Weighted scoring based on Evidence Strength (0-3), Recency (0-3), and Empirical Validation (0-2).

---

## **5. Phase 3: Empirical Validation**
1.  **Layer 1:** Mathematical Stability (asymptotic behavior/overflow).
2.  **Layer 2:** System Stability (inflation/collapse).
3.  **Layer 3:** Player Simulation (Active | Idle | Optimizer | Whale archetypes).

---

## **6. Phase 4: Systematic Reconstruction (Tome v4.0)**
Final authoring of the "Evidence-Backed" tome, modularized by domain and linked directly to Claim IDs.
