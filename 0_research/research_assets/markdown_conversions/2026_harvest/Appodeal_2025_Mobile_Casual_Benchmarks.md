# Appodeal’s 2025 Mobile Casual Benchmarks Report

## Overview
Appodeal’s 2025 Mobile Casual Benchmarks report shows hybrid casual games significantly outperforming hypercasual when it comes to ad-based monetization.

## Key Findings
- While hypercasual games still deliver the biggest number of installs, their advertising revenue per user (ARPU) is much lower than hybrid casual games.
- Hybrid casual games offer deeper engagement mechanics such as progression systems, collection elements, and light RPG or simulation features.
- Hypercasual games have an average ARPU of $0.86.
- Party and Match genre games generate the highest ad revenue, with an ARPU of $4.90 and $2.99, respectively.
- The best-performing subgenre is Merge 3, with an ad ARPU of $14.83.
- Luck Battle games have an ARPU of $12.23.
- Rewarded Video works well in Merge and Idle games (Merge 3: 101.5 videos/user, Idle: 73.2).

## Conclusion
Tanya Moroz, General Manager at Appodeal, notes a continuing shift from hypercasual to hybrid casual games due to better retention and revenue potential.
