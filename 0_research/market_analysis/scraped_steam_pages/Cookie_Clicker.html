Cookie Clicker is a small game where you click a giant cookie to earn cookies, then spend them on buildings and upgrades to earn even more cookies. It's a game of numbers and optimization, with an absurd amount of content.

About This Game

What is Cookie Clicker?

Cookie Clicker is a game about making an absurd amount of cookies. To help you in this endeavor, you can recruit a host of crazy characters, such as:

  • Grandmas
  • Farms
  • Factories
  • Mines
  • Banks
  • Temples
  • Portals
  • Time machines
  • Antimatter condensers
  • Prisms
  • Chancemakers
  • Fractal engines
  • Javascript consoles
  • You
  • And many more...

Features

- Over 600+ upgrades
- Over 500+ achievements
- Cloud saving (no more losing your saves)
- Seasonal events
- Mini-games
- The ability to pet your dragon
- A jazzy soundtrack by C418
- And much more to discover!

Coming in a future update!

More upgrades, more achievements, and more ways to make even more cookies!
