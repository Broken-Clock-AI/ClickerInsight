# 0. Research Phase

This directory contains the foundational data and market intelligence used to inform the design of the prototype.

## Contents
- **`market_analysis/`**: Sentiment reports and scraped data from Google Play and Steam.
- **`research_assets/`**: The core academic corpus.
    - `downloads/`: Raw PDFs and HTML files of academic papers.
    - `markdown_conversions/`: Searchable, text-based versions of the research corpus.

## Data Sources
- **Academic Papers:** 20+ sources spanning ludology, psychology, and mathematics.
- **Market Data:** Analysis of 100+ "Best of" lists and thousands of player reviews for titles like *Egg Inc.* and *Cookie Clicker*.
